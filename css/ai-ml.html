AI & Machine Learning in Economics

Exploring the application of computational techniques to economic questions.

Overview

A significant focus of my research involves leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) techniques to address complex questions in macroeconomics, financial economics, and econometrics. This includes utilizing predictive modeling, exploring computational methods for forecasting, and analyzing large datasets to uncover economic insights.

Below are highlights of my work, skills, and interests in this growing field. Also, check out relevant posts on my Blog.

Relevant Skills

My technical skillset supporting this work includes:

  • **Python:** Libraries such as Scikit-learn, TensorFlow/Keras, PyTorch, Pandas, NumPy for ML model development, data processing, and analysis.
  • **R:** Statistical modeling, econometrics packages, time series analysis, visualization (ggplot2).
  • **Data Handling:** Experience with large dataset manipulation, cleaning, feature engineering, and database querying (SQL).
  • **Econometric Software:** Proficiency in Stata and RATS for traditional econometric modeling and time series forecasting.
