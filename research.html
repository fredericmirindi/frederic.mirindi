Research & Publications

Explore my research interests, publications, and selected conference presentations in economics, econometrics, and AI/ML applications.

Research Interests

  • AI and Machine Learning in Economics: Integration of modern AI/ML techniques in macroeconomic and financial modeling.
  • Econometrics: Causal inference, time series analysis, microeconometrics, and experimental economics.
  • Monetary Policy & DSGE Models: Policy analysis, growth dynamics, and structural modeling.
  • Financial Economics: Risk modeling, asset pricing, and forecasting using advanced quantitative methods.
  • Resource & Development Economics: Historical and contemporary analysis of resource exploitation and economic development, especially in Africa.
