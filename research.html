Research

My research explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and quantitative economics, with applications in macroeconomic and financial modeling.

Research Areas

  • AI/ML in Economics: Integrating machine learning into macroeconomic and financial models
  • Econometrics: Causal inference, time series, microeconometrics
  • Monetary Policy & DSGE Models: Policy analysis, growth dynamics
  • Financial Economics: Risk modeling, asset pricing
  • Resource & Development Economics: Socioeconomic impacts of resource exploitation
