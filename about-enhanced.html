Biography

Frédéric Mirindi is a distinguished PhD Candidate in Economics & Econometrics at the University of Manitoba, recognized as a leading researcher specializing in artificial intelligence applications in economic analysis.

Currently serving as a Lecturer of Microeconomics and Research Associate at Université de Saint Boniface , Frédéric brings extensive experience in machine learning models for economic forecasting and policy analysis. His pioneering work has established innovative approaches to understanding complex economic phenomena through advanced computational methods.

As the Founder of ThinkBit Edge Corp., he bridges the gap between academic research and practical applications, developing cutting-edge AI solutions for economic modeling and analysis. His work contributes significantly to the intersection of technology and economics, advancing both theoretical understanding and practical implementation.
