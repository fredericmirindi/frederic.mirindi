Community Roundtable 2026
Spotlight on the Crises In Sudan And DRC: A Community Roundtable on Imperialism and Global Indifference
Speaker: Johise Namwira, Frédéric Mirindi, and Richard Boli
University of Winnipeg
January 26, 2026 • 10:00 – 11:30 a.m. CST • Room 2M70

Community Roundtable at the University of Winnipeg

Frédéric Mirindi's intervention addressed the following key questions:

  • Shared firsthand perspectives on the ongoing crises and atrocities in Sudan and the DRC
  • Examined the factors behind the general indifference and lack of understanding surrounding these atrocities
  • Addressed the unique challenges faced by members of affected communities living in Canada
  • Articulated why it is essential for Canadians to pay attention to these crises and their global implications
