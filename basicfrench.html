Additional Resources

Explore these additional resources to enrich your French learning experience.

  • FrenchPod101 - Offers a range of French lessons, podcasts, and resources for learners.
  • FluentU - Provides authentic French content with interactive subtitles and language learning tools.
  • iTalki - Connect with native French speakers for language exchange and tutoring.
  • News in Slow French - Improve listening skills with news and stories delivered at a slower pace.

Assess Your French Proficiency

Unsure about your current French proficiency level? Explore these language tests to gauge your skills and identify the ideal learning path for you.

  • Duolingo French Test - Assess your French language skills using Duolingo's comprehensive language proficiency test.
  • Alliance Française Language Tests - Discover the official French language exams and certifications offered by Alliance Française, a trusted institution for French language learning.
  • BBC Languages French Test - Test your knowledge of French with engaging interactive quizzes and resources from BBC Languages.
  • TCF Sample Test (CIEP) - Prepare for the Test de Connaissance du Français (TCF) by practicing with official sample tests from CIEP, a renowned French language testing organization.
