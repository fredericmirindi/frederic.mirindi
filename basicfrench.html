Assess Your French Proficiency

Not sure about your French proficiency level? Take one of these language tests to assess your skills and find the right learning path.

  • Duolingo French Test - Evaluate your French skills with Duolingo's language proficiency test.
  • Alliance Française Language Tests - Discover official French language exams and certifications offered by Alliance Française.
  • BBC Languages French Test - Test your French knowledge with interactive quizzes and resources from BBC Languages.
  • TCF Sample Test (CIEP) - Practice for the Test de Connaissance du Français (TCF) with official sample tests from CIEP.
