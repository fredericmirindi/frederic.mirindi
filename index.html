Advanced Microeconomics

Utility Maximization Problem:

$$\max_{x_1, x_2} U(x_1, x_2) \text{ s.t. } p_1x_1 + p_2x_2 \leq m$$

where \(U(x_1, x_2)\) is the utility function, \(p_1\) and \(p_2\) are prices, and \(m\) is income.

Econometrics

Linear Regression Model:

$$Y_i = \beta_0 + \beta_1X_i + \varepsilon_i$$

where \(Y_i\) is the dependent variable, \(X_i\) is the independent variable, \(\beta_0\) and \(\beta_1\) are coefficients, and \(\varepsilon_i\) is the error term.
